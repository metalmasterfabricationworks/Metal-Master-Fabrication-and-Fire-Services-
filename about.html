About

Metal Master Fabrication & Fire Services is an independently operated fabrication unit based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, delivering metal fabrication and fire-support solutions with a focus on durability, precision, and compliance.

Business Overview

The business operates as a proprietorship and undertakes fabrication work for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Projects range from structural steel fabrication to custom-built metal components and fire-service–related metal works.

Ownership

The firm is owned and managed by Sanjay R. Pandey, who oversees fabrication, execution, and quality control. Direct owner involvement ensures accountability, consistency, and adherence to project requirements.

Location & Operations

The fabrication unit is located at Gala No. 217, New Ashok Nagar, Walbhat Road, Goregaon East, Mumbai Suburban, Maharashtra – 400063. The facility supports fabrication, assembly, and site-dispatch operations.

Compliance

Metal Master Fabrication & Fire Services operates as a GST-registered entity and undertakes work in alignment with applicable safety, structural, and fire-related requirements as specified by project scope.
